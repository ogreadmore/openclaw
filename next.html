FAQ.

Common questions from business owners.

Do I really own it?▼

Yes. Unlike subscribing to software where you lose access if you stop paying, this is built on your own cloud account. We help you set it up and keep it running — but the infrastructure, the data, and the API keys are yours. Walk away anytime and everything keeps working.

What are the ongoing costs?▼

Typical baseline is cloud hosting (like DigitalOcean, usually ~$5-10/month), plus any third-party API usage you choose. We add no vendor markups.

Can you help my marketing agency?▼

Absolutely. We partner with marketing and consulting agencies who want to offer AI agents to their clients without touching the infrastructure. You own the client relationship, we handle the backend. Reach out about agency partnerships — it's one of the most common ways people work with us.

Is this secure?▼

Yes. We deploy with hardened defaults — your agent isn't exposed to the public internet, secrets are managed properly, and access is locked down to only what's needed. It's significantly more secure than a default installation, and our Ops + Security plan adds ongoing patching and monitoring on top.

What about enterprise needs — compliance, SLAs, governance?▼

We've got you covered. Our Custom Care plans include SLAs, environment separation (dev/stage/prod), audit-friendly access controls, release and rollback support, and team onboarding. If you need it production-grade, that's exactly what we do.
